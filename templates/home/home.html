How It Works

Our process is straightforward and hassle-free, making us the best choice for online tailoring in Pakistan.

PLACE YOUR ORDER

Order online to schedule a home tailoring service. Choose your design from our product catalog and book your appointment easily.

Picking Up Your Dress Material

The rider will come at the scheduled time to collect your dress material and the sample dress that fits you perfectly, or to take measurements if needed.

Currently Being Expertly Stitched

Our expert tailors will stitch your dress based on your selected design or one from our product design catalog.

FREE DELIVERY IN 7 DAYS

We’ll ensure your custom-tailored dress and sample dress are carefully packed and securely delivered to you within 7-10 days.

CASH ON DELIVERY
